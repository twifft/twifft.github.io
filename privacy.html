Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how DailyClipzApp ("we", "our", "the App") handles data when using our application.

1. Data We Collect

  • Basic profile information from TikTok (open_id, display name)

2. How We Use Your Data

We only use TikTok-provided data to upload videos to a user's TikTok drafts.

3. Data Storage

No personal data is stored on any external server. Temporary tokens are discarded immediately after use.
